About the AFRO NFP Knowledge Network
The Africa IHR NFP Knowledge Network was formed in 2018 and has since provided technical stewardship to peer learning, knowledge sharing and technical resources geared at strengthening capacities of IHR NFPs, stakeholders and other IHR related sectors within the dynamic humanitarian crisis settings of countries in Africa- Fragile Conflict and Vulnerable states and Small Island Developing States. The Network provides peer learning support for NFPs through three languages: English, French and Portuguese which cover the entire African Continent.
