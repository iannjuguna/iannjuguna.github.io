EPIC Project Implementation Guide
This guide was created to present the EPIC game project goal, objectives, long term plan and development steps taken on the road to getting the final versions of the game. It is designed to be a manual for users of the game who are implementing a training with an emphasis on public health preparedness, readiness and IHR (2005) compliance; providing all the needed steps and files required to have a successful EPIC game session with the target audience.
