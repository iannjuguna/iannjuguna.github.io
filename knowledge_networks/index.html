About the Readiness Knowledge Network
The Readiness Knowledge Network (a network of networks) seeks to bring together NFP professionals and associated stakeholders, including actors in the humanitarian sector (Fragile, Conflict and Vulnerable States- FCVs & Small Island Developing States- SIDS) the Global Health Cluster, to collaborate and learn from one another and from each other's contexts to bolster international cooperation and better support IHR implementation and operational bout readiness. Its focus is on National IHR Focal Points (NFPs) & IHR Stakeholders including those in sectors beyond Health.
