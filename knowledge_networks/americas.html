About the Americas NFP Knowledge Network
Established in October 2019, the PAHO Knowledge Network seeks to provide a peer to peer learning platforms, knowledge sharing activities both virtual and inperson and technical resources that enhance IHR Implementation by NFPs and other IHR stakeholders in the WHO Americas region. The Network connects NFPs in the following languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese and French.
